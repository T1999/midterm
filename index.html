Top 3 Action Movies

My choices for 3 of my favorite action movies and why I chose them!

The Bourne Identity

Jason Bourne and his journey to find out who he really is.

The Bourne Identity is one of my favorite action movies due to the way Matt Damon portrays himself as a skilled fighter and an extraordinary individual. Throughout the movie, Jason seeks to discover who he really is and that is relatable to me as I feel like I am always trying to figure out who I am and if I have something to identify my self with.

American Sniper

Chris Kyle and his fight to find his real purpose in life.

In this movie, Chris Kyle is an extraordinary sniper who serves in Iraq and saves many lives but despite being seen as a hero, he wants to go back to the military. The movie resonates with me because I feel as though Chris Kyle felt a special connection to the military and despite having a wife and kids, he felt something calling him to serve alongside his brothers.

Transformers 1

In Transformers 1, two types of robots fight for the future of humanity, one for good and one for evil.

In this movie, two types of robots have brought their war to Earth. The main characters, Sam Witwicky and Mikaela Banes, get involved with the good robots in the fight to save humanity from extinction. This movie is one of my favorites of all time because of how realistic the concept of robots coming to Earth is portrayed. Unlike the newer transformer movies, the first one didnt invlove as much cgi animation. A lot of scenes revolved around humans and there was a certain line that wasn't crossed in terms of involving the concept of the robots. The director just made the possibility of robots interacting with humas so real and did a great job.
